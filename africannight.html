African Night: Celebrating African Culture

Join us for the African Students Association's biggest event of the year, African Night! Experience a vibrant celebration of African culture through captivating performances, delectable food, and rhythmic music.

Get Tickets

Enthralling Performances

Immerse yourself in a night of unforgettable performances showcasing the rich diversity of African talent. From traditional dances to contemporary music, our lineup of artists will captivate and inspire.

  • Traditional African Dances
  • Afrobeats Music Performance
  • Parade of Flags
  • Fashion Show

Culinary Delights

Indulge in the flavors of Africa with a diverse array of delicious dishes from different regions. Our talented chefs will craft a menu that showcases the vibrant and authentic tastes of African cuisine.

Live Music and Dance

The pulsating beats and infectious rhythms of African music will set the stage ablaze, inviting you to let loose and dance the night away. Our talented DJs will curate a playlist that combines traditional African tunes with contemporary hits.

Get ready to move your feet to the:

  • Enchanting Afrobeats (West Africa)
  • Maghreb Rai (North Africa)
  • Amapiano (Southern Africa)
  • Soukous (Central Africa)
  • Bongo Flava (East Africa) and many more
